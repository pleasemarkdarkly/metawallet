Empowering phones. Empowering customers.

MetaWallet facilitates low-value transactions across carrier networks using open standards based on SMS functionalities. With MetaWallet, carriers can differentiate their services and become more competitive by increasing ARPU, decreasing churn, and attracting new subscribers.

The MetaWallet solution audits all transactions and supports person-to-person, as well as point-of-sale transactions. It is available to anyone with a mobile phone.

A Bank In Your Pocket

MetaWallet converts the mobile phone into a “mobile wallet,” affording wireless subscribers secure and convenient access to their money. MetaWallet enables subscribers to easily:

  • Purchase Minutes: From their phone, wireless subscribers can purchase and add minutes to their account, as well as send minutes to another subscriber.
  • Transfer Money: Subscribers can send and receive money from abroad.
  • Send Peer-to-Peer Payments: Subscribers can transfer money from their account to another subscriber’s mobile account or bank account and create short codes for regular payment transactions.
  • Pay Bills: Subscribers can pay bills and establish regular, automated payment schedules.
  • Conduct Bank Transactions: Subscribers can access their bank account to transfer money between accounts, check balances etc.
  • Buy Added Features: Subscribers can purchase and add features to their phone such as games, ringtones and music.
