FAQs
Frequently asked questions:
  • What is MetaWallet?
MetaWallet uses encrypted text messages on your cell phone to complete point of sale, peer to peer, and traditional banking transactions. It is a safe, secure, fast and easy way to get more value from your bank and your cell phone.
  • Who can use MetaWallet?
Your cell phone carrier and your bank may already offer MetaWallet. Contact your carrier for details.
  • How much does it cost to use MetaWallet?
MetaWallet costs vary by bank and cell phone carrier. Typically, mobile banking services are offered at a per transaction fee or by subscription.
  • Is MetaWallet secure?
Yes. Your MetaWallet account has redundant security measures to ensure your money is safe. MetaWallet uses the highest international security standards available for mobile banking. The security system includes encrypted SMS over the air ensuring your transaction's privacy and integrity.
  • How do I sign up for MetaWallet?
You need to contact your cellular service provider to sign up for MetaWallet. Some phones with data connections can download MetaWallet software.
  • What are the benefits of MetaWallet?
MetaWallet gives you the control you want over your money anywhere you have cellular service. If you can send a text message you can transfer funds, check account balances, pay bills and enjoy many other great features offered by MetaWallet.
