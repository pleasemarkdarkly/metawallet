Security

MetaWallet Security Features
MetaWallet uses the highest international security standards available for mobile banking. The security system includes encrypted SMS over the air ensuring your transaction´s privacy and integrity. A 4-Digit PIN number makes your MetaWallet account only accessible by you.

If your mobile phone is stolen or you suspect fraudulent activity on your MetaWallet account, block your MetaWallet account using your SIM-menu or call our help centre to block your MetaWallet Account.

  Security Aspect Benefits  
  • Encrypted SMS messages
   Allows total privacy and integrity of your sent SMS messages.
  • 4-digit PIN number
   Allows only you to access you Mobile Wallet Account
