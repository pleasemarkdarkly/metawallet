How to Sign Up with your Mobile?
Signing up with your mobile phone is easy! Just follow the instructions set below and make sure your name entered is the same as your name on your Identity Card. And do not forget to deposit money on your account before making transactions!

Option 1 (Smart with SIM Toolkit):
  1. In your SMART cell phone, go to Smart Menu and click Smart Money
  2. Choose Activate Card
  3. Enter your desired 6-digit w-PIN twice
  4. You will receive a confirmation message with your activated 16-digit SMART Money Number

Option 2 (GCash):
  1. send REG(space)4-digit M-PIN/Mother's Maiden Name/First Name/Last Name/Address/Tel No. to 2882

Option 3 (MTN):
  1. Go to a registered outlet and buy a Mobile Wallet Pack (includes SIM)
