Empowering Access.
Empowering Customers.

MetaWallet facilitates low-value financial transactions across carrier networks and gives mobile phone users access to their bank accounts while on the go. With MetaWallet, users can securely conduct routine banking operations such as checking account balances, making payments, or transferring funds.

Using unique MetaWallet functionality, financial institutions can attract new customers and increase their customer base by offering the convenience and flexibility of secure mobile banking.

Secure, Convenient Transactions

With MetaWallet, banks of all sizes can quickly and cost-effectively enter the mobile payments market, providing new and existing customers a convenient way to access their money.

MetaWallet supports person-to-person transactions offering users an easy, convenient method to quickly send money to anyone with a mobile phone. MetaWallet also supports person-to-merchant transactions, giving mobile phone users the ability to purchase goods and services from participating merchants.

MetaWallet’s solutions integrate with bank and carrier infrastructures, and include auditing of all transactions.

With MetaWallet, banks can:

  • Increase customer satisfaction by offering customers secure, easy access to money anywhere, anytime.
  • Attract new customers, including the highly mobile "unbanked" and lucrative youth markets.
  • Become more competitive and build a brand as a provider of innovative new services.
