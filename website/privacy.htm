Privacy Policy
MetaWallet takes your privacy seriously.

1. Introduction
MetaWallet, Inc. is committed to providing a safe and private environment to store, download, share, and print your contact information. This Privacy Statement covers how MetaWallet collects, uses, and shares information. The terms of this Privacy Statement are considered part of MetaWallet's Terms of Service. You can review the most current version of the Terms of Service at: http://www.MetaWallet.us/privacy

2. Information Collection
MetaWallet collects information when you register and use our services. In order to establish your account, we ask for personal information such as your name, email address, mobile phone serial number, mobile phone number, mobile phone service provider, and preferred language. You may edit your personal information via the MetaWallet Profile page at any time. See “Access to Personal Information.”

In order to upload contact information to your mobile telephone, we ask for information such as a contact name and phone number. You may edit contact information via the MetaWallet Contacts page at any time.

MetaWallet collects information about your transactions with the MetaWallet service, as well as information received from your browser, such as your IP address, cookie information, and pages you request from MetaWallet web servers. MetaWallet sets and accesses cookies on your computer when you use MetaWallet services. We use cookies to store user preferences, such as language settings or mobile phone service provider. We also assign you a unique ID and store it on your computer in the form of a cookie to personalize our site to your profile. We also store a cookie relating to you on our servers, however our cookie does not contain any personal information.

3. Information Use
MetaWallet uses collected information to fulfill your requests for MetaWallet services, to contact you for service related topics, to improve our services, and to provide billing-related reports to MetaWallet partner companies.

When requested by you, contact information is uploaded to your mobile phone, uploaded to a third party mobile phone (e.g., friend's phone), or sent to an email address you provide (e.g., friend's email address). If the phone number or email address you provide does not match with an existing MetaWallet account, an invitation in the form of a Short Message Service (SMS) or email message is sent to the phone number or email address provided. For our referral function, we may send one or more invitational communications on your behalf. This is stored only to track the success of our referral function. To remove any unregistered invitee from our system contact us at help@MetaWallet.us

MetaWallet reserves the right to send you service and administrative messages without offering you the opportunity to opt-out of receiving these messages. Service and administrative messages include, among other things, a welcome message, profile change messages, temporary password assignment messages, contact list messages, free upload notification messages, change of Terms of Service notification messages, and change of privacy policy notification messages.

If you check the opt-in check box on the profile page, MetaWallet may occasionally send you information about new products, special deals, or promotions.

MetaWallet may send you strictly service-related announcements on rare occasions when it is necessary to do so. For instance, if our service is temporarily suspended for maintenance, we might send you an email. You may not opt-out of these communications, which are not promotional in nature. If you do not wish to receive them, you have the option to deactivate your account.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure
MetaWallet does not share, rent, or sell collected information with other people or companies except when we:

Have your permission via an opt-in process;

Provide billing-related information to third party partners- in which case they are not permitted to use the information for secondary purposes;

Respond to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process as required by law;

Need to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims;

Need to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, or violations of MetaWallet's terms of service;

Provide aggregate information that does not include personally identifiable data for statistical analysis of user behavior to third party partners; or

Are acquired by or merged with another company.

MetaWallet sends service and administrative messages (such as contact information, a verification code, and alert messages) in the form of an SMS message, through one or more mobile telephone service provider networks (i.e., network operators). Please contact your mobile telephone service provider for terms of use (including any charges that you may incur) and privacy statements related to your transmission and receipt of SMS messages.

5. Information Security
Information is password protected and is not visible to other members of the MetaWallet service, unless you explicitly share it. Your password is stored in an encrypted manner. MetaWallet uses industry-standard SSL-encryption to protect data transmissions between your browser and our servers.

MetaWallet has physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard collected information. Except as otherwise stated in this Privacy Statement, access to the information is limited to MetaWallet employees or contractors who need to come in contact with the information in order to do their jobs.

6. De-Activating Your MetaWallet Account
If you want to de-activate your MetaWallet account, please send an email to help@MetaWallet.us with your request. Include the username, email address, telephone number, and telephone serial number of the account you wish to de-activate. Your account will be de-activated and you will no longer receive communications from us beyond the de-activation message. However, we will keep your account information in our database for record keeping, billing, and statistical analysis purposes only.

7. Access to Personally Identifiable Information
You may change your personally identifiable information by making the change via the MetaWallet Profile page or by emailing our help@MetaWallet.us or by contacting us by telephone or postal mail at the contact information listed below.

When you change your information via the Profile page your changes take effect immediately and when you submit changes to your information via email or phone, we will respond to your request within 10-30 business days.

8. Changes and Updates
MetaWallet reserves the right to modify this Privacy Statement from time to time by posing a new version, without notice to you, at http://www.Metawallet.us/privacy.htm. We will notify you about significant changes to our Privacy Statement via email messages to the address specified in your MetaWallet account or by placing a notice on our site. MetaWallet will not use collected information in a materially new way without first providing you with an opportunity to opt-out.
