MetaWallet: Mobile Banking for Emerging Markets

MetaWallet has married banking and cellular technology to enable consumers to conduct financial transactions on their cell phones. MetaWallet’s innovative, carrier-centric approach to mobile banking creates significant new revenue sources for carriers and banks, and brings new service and convenience to mobile banking customers. MetaWallet’s software solution is specially designed to address the unique needs in emerging markets.
